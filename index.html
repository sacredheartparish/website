Welcome to the parishes of Sacred Heart and St. Bridget

Serving the communities of Perth, Lanark, Stanleyville, Ferguson Falls, and McDonalds Corners.

Please do not hesitate to contact the parish office if we can be of assistance to you or others in any way. We are always available to respond to a request for prayer, the sacrament of confession 30 minutes before each Mass and/or conversation outside the rectory by appointment contact: Father Charles Enyinnia at frenyinnia@yahoo.com or 613-328-8331 or Associate Pastor Father Brian Russell fr.brian.russell@gmail.com or 613-267-1043.
